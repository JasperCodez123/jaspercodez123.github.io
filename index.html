Frontline

Frontlines are the characters that fight on point. They protect teammates!

Damage

Damage dealers are the characters that take down frontlines. They have high dps!

Support

Supports are the characters that heal their allies. They are very important!

Flank

Flankers are the characters that kill damage dealers and supports. They have high mobility!
