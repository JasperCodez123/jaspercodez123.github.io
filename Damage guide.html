Damage Guide

Hi, this is a beginner guide on damage dealers in paladins

Click here if you are new to paladins!

Viktor

Viktor is a middle range damage dealer. He has one of the highest DPS in the whole game. Try to flank him as he his weak when being flanked.

Cassie

Cassie is a high mobility damage dealer. She is best used at middle range because she shoots projectiles. Try to apply cc on her to make her very vulnerable.

Sha Lin

Sha lin is a backline damage dealer. Sha Lin's impale + planted combo is very good against flanks. Try to stay as mobile as possible to catch Sha Lin off guard

Kinessa

Kinessa is a backline damage dealer. Kinessa is good at dealing airborne targets. Try to flank her as she is weak when being flanked.

Drogoz

Drogoz is a high mobility damage dealer and excels at destroying shields. Drogoz damage output is very high. Try to use hitscan characters to counter him.

Tyra

Tyra is good at zoning tanks off the point. Her damage output is similar to viktor which means it is very high. Try to flank her as she doesn't have any movement abilities.

Willo

Willo is a great anti-deathball comp damage dealer. She is good at zoning tanks off the point. Try to flank her as her auto attack isn't the best.
